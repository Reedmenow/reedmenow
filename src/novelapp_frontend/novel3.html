In a small coastal town, Clara ran a quaint little bakery named “Sweet Dreams.” Each morning, the smell of fresh bread and pastries wafted through the air, attracting locals and tourists alike. But despite the joy she brought to others, Clara felt a lingering emptiness in her heart after her fiancé left her for a life of adventure.

Page 2

As they talked, Clara learned that Leo was on a quest to rediscover his passion for photography. His eyes sparkled with enthusiasm, reminding her of the dreams she had once chased.

Page 3

One evening, while walking along the beach, Leo stopped and took Clara’s hands in his. “You’ve brought light back into my life,” he said softly. “I want to share my journey with you, not apart from you.”
