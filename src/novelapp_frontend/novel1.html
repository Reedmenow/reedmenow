Page 1

In the quiet town of Eldridge, shadows lingered longer than they should. Sarah, a young journalist, returned to her hometown after her mother’s sudden passing, determined to uncover the secrets that lay hidden within its cobblestone streets. As she walked through the familiar alleys, memories of her childhood flickered like candlelight in the dark corners of her mind.

Page 2

The librarian, an elderly woman named Mrs. Harper, greeted her with a knowing smile. “Looking for something specific, dear?” “Just some old stories,” Sarah replied, scanning the shelves filled with forgotten tales. Mrs. Harper handed her a dusty book titled “Legends of Eldridge.”

Page 3

Days turned into weeks, and Sarah pieced together fragments of a dark history—betrayal, lost loves, and unsolved mysteries. One night, as the moon hung low, she heard a whisper in the wind, drawing her to the old cemetery.
